# üìã PRESENTATION TIPS

## Timing (8 minutes total)
- Slides 1-2: 1 minute (problem + solution)
- Slides 3-5: 4.5 minutes (3 demos)
- Slides 6-12: 2.5 minutes (proof, business, ask)
- Questions: 2+ minutes

## Delivery
- **Talk to people, not slides**
- **Pause after demos for effect**
- **Make eye contact**
- **Speak slowly, clearly**
- **Show confidence, not arrogance**

## Body Language
- Stand, don't sit
- Use hands to emphasize points
- Smile when appropriate
- Don't fidget
- Own the room

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ‚ùå Reading from slides
- ‚ùå Going over time
- ‚ùå Talking too fast
- ‚ùå Getting defensive on questions
- ‚ùå Forgetting to ask for the money

## If Demo Breaks
- Stay calm
- Say: "Let me show you yesterday's recording"
- Play backup video
- Continue confidently
- Offer to debug after
